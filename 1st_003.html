Be female.

The majors will be STEM.

Enroll in the first semester of the master's program in the fall of 2025 and cannot be deferred students.

The universities you are admitted to has to be listed in all or almost all of the top 10 of major university rankings.

Have Chinese nationality.

Be 25 or less by the end of 2025.

Applicants can click "Start Application" on this page, fill in the basic information, and click "Submit Application". You need not submit any documents at the first stage. After our preliminary review, we will email the applicants who we choose an notification to submit additional information.

3. Deadline for submitting applications

February 7, 2025, before the 2 pm, GMT+8.

4. Date for final decision

September 1, 2025,before the 2 pm, GMT+8. On that day, we will only send out the notification to the student we decide to offer the scholarship.
